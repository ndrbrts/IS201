About Me

This is who I am

My name is Nick Roberts. I was born and raised in Kaysville, Utah. I served a 2 year mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day-Saints in Lima Peru. I am currently a full time student at Brigham Young University as well as working full time. I married Mary Jones in September of 2020. We currently live in Bountiful, Utah. I am working on getting a degree in accounting. Mary is going to school at UVU to become a dental hygienist.

We enjoy many different activities together. We love to go boating in the summer. We go on an annual trip to Lake Powell every August. My wife is a huge Ute fan and we also like going to the Ute football games.
